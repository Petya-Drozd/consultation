My approach

Individually tailored

No single psychological state is the same, nor the patient. This is why my professional goal is to tailor my approach in counseling to the unique set of circumstances of any individual patient.

Non-patronizing

In my practice I never underestimate and I always assess any life struggles as those that are understood not as problems to be fixed or eradicated, but rather as important indications & knowledge that must be used in the healing process.

Strengths-focused

My stance is that everyone has both a set of weaknesses and a set of strengths. As a counseling professional, I will do my best to eliminate the former and to empower the latter. Any person that seeks therapy has a set of strengths.

Collaborative

My personal perception of the relationship between a therapist and a patient is that of an equal partnership with shared motivations and goals. I facilitate and help support a process we co-create together.

Experience-based

I’ve been in the counseling business for years upon years. And let me tell you one thing - every single person and every single new patient enrich my experience and understanding of my craft.

Holistic

As someone formal medical training, I am convinced that any issue should be taken holistically. Body, heart, mind, and spirit are all equally important here.

FAQ

What is a typical therapy appointment?
Appointments usually last about 45 minutes. The sessions are held once or twice a week which depends on your special situation. You should attend the therapies regularly, come on time, and cancel in only urgent cases.
How frequently will I visit you?
During our initial consultation, we'll discuss the schedule that fits your needs the best. Typically, we'll set up a specific date and time to meet once or twice a week. Based on your treatment, a higher frequency of sessions may be offered.
What benefits can I expect from working with a therapist?
Psychotherapy sessions offer plenty of benefits. Counseling therapies help save and improve the lives of many people struggling with mental health disorders. Mental health counseling treatments can accentuate your background, feelings and emotions, thoughts and behavior, or a mix of all these elements. Looking for counseling is a feature of spirit and power because it's a considerable step to start control your mental health and build the life you dreamed of, a life which is worth living.
